History

Goodness Care Pharmacy, was established on June 15, 2020. The pharmacy starts with a simple and humble beginning.Just after two years, it grows to be a trusted pharmacy offering reasonably priced and quality medicines. The owner said, "I can say that my experience in medicine distribution has affected my desire to pursue establishing a small pharmacy someday". She worked as a distributor of medicines for five years. Working in this field for many years made her realize the unceasing needs of people for medicines. There has been a continuous increase in demands for medicine among people and these demands need to be met. So that the people can get well and lives healthier. Goodness Care Pharmacy, started as a business plan that she made as part of her course requirements. After working as a distributor, She decided to study Business Management. Since owning a pharmacy has always been her dream, she decided to make this the subject of her business plan and eventually she realized that her business plan is slowly falling into place. The challenges brought by the pandemic have drastically affected the business but these did not stop her from achieving her goal of not just establishing her own business but also helping people by providing them a reliable source of quality medicines at affordable prices. In serving local residents of Bagtas, Tanza, Cavite. Goodness Care Pharmacy is committed to providing quality products and friendly services at all times. Its staffs are willing to assist its customers in all of their needs including maintenance medicine needs, other related health needs, and wellness needs. Accuracy, quality, and reliability are the core values that GCP won’t ever compromise.

Mission

We aim to help the people by giving them a chance to have a better life, by providing the most efficient and reliable quality prooven products we can help them economically and physically.

Vision

Our vision is to continualy grow and achieve stability in pharmacy industry and to be the leading provider of good quality of medicine here in Cavite and throughout the country.
